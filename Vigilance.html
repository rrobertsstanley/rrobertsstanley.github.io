How to Play

⊹
Stars appear on a 9×9 grid. Eliminate each star by entering its coordinates before more appear.

Yellow priority stars accumulate score at double the rate — eliminate these first.
↓
Lower score is better. Each star scores 0.1 pts per 0.1s it remains on screen. Priority stars score at 2×.
